What Do You Get When You Listen to Authentic Praise?

We start off our week with our Monday prayer and we recharge, rejuvenate, and rejoice in a word from Sunday. Then we are "true to ourselves" on Tuesday. We acknowledge the good things that come to those of us who wait on Wednesday, and are thankful for our Thursdays. All of this while incorporating a motivational minute and food for thought. We can't end the week without praying for our family and acknowledging what’s up with our weekend.
